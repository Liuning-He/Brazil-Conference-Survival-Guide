# Emergency Card / 紧急自救卡


> **Print this card and carry a paper copy with you. Do not keep it with your phone, passport, wallet, or other valuables. Make sure this is the one thing you are most likely to still have in a crisis.**
>
> **请打印这张卡片并随身携带纸质版。不要把它和手机、护照、钱包或其他贵重物品放在一起。确保在危机情况下，这张纸是你最有可能还保留下来的东西。**
