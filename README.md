A practical bilingual guide to staying safe and prepared at conferences in Brazil.

一份面向赴巴西参会者的双语实用指南，重点覆盖安全、支付、通信、交通，以及被盗抢后的紧急自救。

## Who this is for / 适用对象

- International attendees going to conferences in Brazil
- First-time visitors to Rio or other major Brazilian cities
- Travelers who want a concrete backup plan before arrival

- 前往巴西参加会议的国际参会者
- 第一次去里约或其他巴西主要城市的人
- 希望在出发前准备一套明确备用方案的旅行者

## What’s inside / 包含内容

- Before-you-go checklist / 出发前核对清单
- Airport arrival and transport / 落地与交通
- Cash, cards, eSIM, and internet / 现金、银行卡、eSIM 与网络
- Hotel and daily safety habits / 酒店与日常安全习惯
- Conference-day precautions / 参会当天注意事项
- Emergency recovery after theft or robbery / 被盗抢后的紧急自救

## Core principle / 核心原则

**Personal safety comes first. Property is replaceable.**

**人身安全优先，财物永远排在后面。**

## Why this repo / 为什么写这个仓库

Conference travel to Brazil can be exciting, but also stressful if you arrive unprepared. This repo is meant to be a calm, practical, and shareable resource for attendees.

去巴西参会可能很精彩，但如果准备不足，也会非常被动。这个仓库希望提供一份冷静、实用、可转发的参会资源。

## Disclaimer / 说明

This guide is based on practical travel judgment and shared attendee experience. It is not legal or consular advice.

本指南基于实际旅行判断与参会者经验整理，不构成法律、警方或领事建议。
