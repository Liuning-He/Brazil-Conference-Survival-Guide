## 1. Before You Go / 出发前

### 1.1 Cards / 银行卡

#### Bottom line / 一句话结论

**至少准备两张实体卡：1 张主卡，1 张备卡。主力优先用 Visa 或 Mastercard。不要把 Amex 或银联当唯一支付方式。**

Bring at least two physical cards: one primary card and one backup card. Make Visa or Mastercard your main payment path. Do not rely on Amex or UnionPay as your only option.

#### Recommended setup / 推荐配置

- **身上主钱包**：1 张主信用卡（优先 Visa 或 Mastercard）  
  **On you**: 1 primary credit card (preferably Visa or Mastercard)

- **酒店房间 / 行李深处**：1 张备用信用卡，最好来自**不同银行**  
  **In your room / deep in luggage**: 1 backup credit card, ideally from a different bank

- **手机钱包**：最好绑 1 张卡，作为第三支付路径  
  **In your phone wallet**: Add 1 card if possible, as a third payment path

- **推荐再带 1 张可境外 ATM 取现的借记卡**  
  Add 1 debit card that works for overseas ATM withdrawals

#### Do not / 不要这样做

- 不要只带 **1 张卡**  
  Do not travel with only one card

- 不要把 **所有卡都放在同一个钱包**  
  Do not keep all your cards in the same wallet

- 不要把 **主卡、护照、手机** 放在同一个容易被抢走的小包里  
  Do not put your main card, passport, and phone in the same easy-to-snatch bag

- 不要默认 **Amex / 银联一定到处都能顺畅刷**  
  Do not assume Amex / UnionPay will work everywhere without friction

#### Notes / 备注

- 对普通参会者来说，**Visa / Mastercard 更适合作为主力国际支付路径**。  
  For most attendees, Visa / Mastercard are the safer primary international payment path.

- **银联在巴西并不是完全不能用**，但更适合作为补充，而不是唯一依赖。  
  UnionPay is not unusable in Brazil, but it is better treated as a backup path rather than your only one.

- 如果刷卡机让你选币种，**选 BRL（巴西雷亚尔）**。  
  If the payment terminal asks which currency to use, choose **BRL (Brazilian real)**.

### 1.2 Cash / 现金

#### Bottom line / 一句话结论

**带少量现金，但不要带太多。以小面额为主，把现金当应急工具，不要当主要支付方式。**

Carry some cash, but not too much. Prefer small notes. Treat cash as a backup tool, not your main payment method.

#### Recommended setup / 推荐配置

- **主钱包**：当天要用的小额现金  
  **In your main wallet**: a small amount for the day

- **备用位置**：再放一小笔备用现金  
  **In a separate backup spot**: another small emergency amount

- **大额现金**：不建议  
  **Large cash holdings**: not recommended

#### Recommended denominations / 建议面值

- **总面值：R$300-500 就够用**  

- **优先准备**: R$10 / R$20 / R$50

- **少量即可**: R$5 / R$100

- **不建议带 R$200**: 太大，不利于找零，也更容易显得你带了较多现金。  
  Too large, awkward for change, and can make it look like you are carrying more cash than you should.

#### What Brazilian cash looks like / 巴西现金大概是什么样

- 常见纸币面值包括 Common banknote denominations include:
R$2 / R$5 / R$10 / R$20 / R$50 / R$100 / R$200

- 常见硬币包括 Common coins include:
R$1 / 50 centavos / 25 centavos / 10 centavos / 5 centavos

#### Why cash still matters / 为什么还是要带一点现金

- 在一些小额消费场景，现金更直接，也能减少小商户刷卡金额出错或被改金额的风险  
  Cash is simpler for some small purchases and reduces the risk of card amount mistakes or tampering at small merchants

- 手机没电、卡片临时风控、网络不稳时，现金是保底路径  
  Cash is a fallback when your phone dies, your card is blocked, or the network is unstable

- 真遇到突发情况时，手里有一点小额现金，会比只有刷不了的卡更从容  
  In an unexpected situation, having a small amount of cash is better than depending only on cards that may not work

#### How to carry and use cash / 现金怎么带、怎么用

- 只带当天要用的量，并以小面额为主  
  Carry only what you expect to need that day, and prefer smaller notes

- 主钱包里只放当前要花的现金，备用现金分开放
  Keep only spending cash in your main wallet, and store backup cash separately

- 付款前先把大致金额准备好，不要站在路边慢慢翻现金或找零  
  Get your cash roughly ready before paying, and do not count through notes slowly in public

- 不要带一大叠现金出门，也不要把全部现金放在同一个地方  
  Do not go out carrying a thick wad of cash or keep all your cash in one place

#### ATM use / 怎么提现更稳

- 把 ATM 提现当备用方案，不要当日常习惯  
  Treat ATM cash withdrawals as a backup option, not a daily routine

- 优先在机场、商场、银行网点、酒店等室内环境提现  
  Prefer ATMs in airports, malls, bank branches, hotels, or other indoor settings

- 不要在街边、夜间、或明显让你觉得不对劲的环境里提现  
  Avoid using exposed street ATMs, especially at night or when the surroundings feel wrong

- 取完钱立刻收好，不要原地数钱  
  Put the money away immediately and do not count it in public

### 1.3 Connectivity, Phone & Accounts / 网络、手机与关键账号

#### Bottom line / 一句话结论

**出发前先把上网方案和手机失窃后的补救路径准备好。至少保留两条联网路径：主手机 + 备用方案；不要把最关键的验证码和恢复手段全绑在同一台手机上**

Sort out both connectivity and phone-loss recovery before departure. Keep two ways to get online: your main phone plus one backup path. Do not tie every recovery method and verification path to the same phone.

#### Recommended setup / 推荐配置

- 上网方案优先级：国际漫游能直接用就最省心；能用 eSIM 的手机，提前装好一张可在巴西使用的数据方案也很实用；如果两者都没有，再落地后处理本地 SIM  
  Roaming is the simplest if your carrier plan already works well abroad. If your phone supports eSIM, installing a Brazil-capable data plan before departure is also practical. If neither works, sort out a local SIM after arrival.

- 至少准备两条联网路径：主手机网络 + 酒店 Wi-Fi / 备用 eSIM / 备用手机中的一种  
  Keep at least two connectivity paths: your main mobile connection plus one backup such as hotel Wi-Fi, a backup eSIM, or a second phone.

- 关键账号只看这几类：邮箱、银行 App、WhatsApp、微信、Apple ID / Google 账号  
  Treat these as your critical accounts: email, banking apps, WhatsApp, WeChat, and your Apple ID / Google account.

#### Before you leave / 出发前先做完

- 确认手机是否支持 eSIM，并在出发前把 eSIM 或国际漫游开通好  
  Check whether your phone supports eSIM, and set up either eSIM or international roaming before departure.

- 把酒店地址、会场地址、航班信息、保险信息离线保存到手机里  
  Save your hotel address, venue address, flight details, and insurance information offline on your phone.

- 开启 Find My iPhone 或 Find My Device，并确认自己会远程锁机  
  Turn on Find My iPhone or Find My Device, and make sure you know how to lock your phone remotely.

- 给邮箱、银行、WhatsApp、微信、Apple ID / Google 账号开好验证和找回方式  
  Set up verification and recovery options for email, banking, WhatsApp, WeChat, and your Apple ID / Google account.

- **不要把最关键的验证码和恢复手段全绑在同一台手机上**  
  Do not tie every recovery method and verification path to the same phone.

#### Good habits / 日常使用建议

- 落地后先确认三件事：手机有网、打车软件能用、酒店地址能离线打开  
  After landing, check three things first: you have data, your ride-hailing app works, and your hotel address is available offline.

- 非必要不要在街边长时间拿着手机查地图、回消息或刷会议信息  
  Avoid standing on the street using your phone for long stretches to check maps, messages, or conference details.

- 会场外尽量不要边走边看手机  
  Outside the venue, avoid walking while looking at your phone.

- 重要联系人、酒店电话、同伴电话，至少保留一份离线记录  
  Keep an offline copy of important contacts, your hotel number, and a companion's phone number.

#### If your phone is stolen / 如果手机被抢或丢了

- 先锁手机，不要先慌着逐个处理小账号  
  Lock the phone first. Do not start by dealing with minor accounts one by one.

- 优先保护邮箱和银行，因为它们通常是其他账号的恢复入口  
  Prioritize your email and banking access, because they are often the recovery path for other accounts.

- 尽快联系运营商封停原号码或原 SIM，并补办新卡  
  Contact your mobile carrier as soon as possible to block the original number or SIM and get a replacement.

- 再处理 WhatsApp、微信和其他高频通信账号  
  Then deal with WhatsApp, WeChat, and other high-use communication accounts.

#### Notes / 备注

- 对大多数参会者来说，国际漫游最省心；eSIM 更适合想在出发前就把数据方案准备好的人  
  For most attendees, roaming is the easiest option; eSIM is better for people who want to set up data before departure.

- 真出事时，先锁手机，再守邮箱和银行，再恢复通信  
  If something goes wrong, lock the phone first, then secure email and banking, then restore communications.

### 1.4 Documents & Backup / 证件与备份

#### Bottom line / 一句话结论

**不要把护照、主手机、主银行卡和全部现金放在一起。关键证件至少保留一份离线副本和一份云端或联系人备份，这样真出事时你还能证明身份、联系酒店、补办证件、继续行程。**

Do not keep your passport, main phone, main bank card, and all your cash together. Keep at least one offline copy and one cloud or trusted-contact backup of your key documents so you can still prove your identity, contact your hotel, replace documents, and continue your trip if something goes wrong.

#### Recommended setup / 推荐配置

- 实体证件分两层：护照随身，其他不常用的重要文件尽量放在住处  
  Split physical documents into two layers: keep your passport on you, and leave other less frequently needed important papers in your room whenever possible.

- 数字备份分两层：手机离线保存一份，云端或可信联系人再存一份  
  Keep digital backups in two layers: one offline copy on your phone, plus another in the cloud or with a trusted contact.

- 重要信息不要只保存图片，最好再单独存一份纯文字版本  
  Do not rely only on photos. Save a plain-text version of key information as well.

#### What to back up / 建议备份什么

- 护照照片页  
  Passport ID page

- 签证或入境相关文件（如适用）  
  Visa or entry-related documents, if applicable

- 航班信息  
  Flight details

- 酒店名称、地址、电话  
  Hotel name, address, and phone number

- 会场名称、地址  
  Venue name and address

- 旅行保险信息  
  Travel insurance information

- 紧急联系人信息  
  Emergency contact information

- 银行卡最后四位和发卡行客服联系方式  
  Last four digits of your cards and your bank contact information

#### Before you leave / 出发前先做完

- 把护照、签证、航班、酒店、保险信息保存到手机相册和文件夹里，并确认离线可打开  
  Save your passport, visa, flight, hotel, and insurance information to your phone and make sure they are available offline.

- 把酒店地址和会场地址单独记成纯文字，避免只有截图却搜不到  
  Save your hotel and venue addresses as plain text, not just screenshots.

- 把一份关键材料发给可信联系人，至少让一个人能在你出事时远程帮你找回信息  
  Send one copy of your key documents to a trusted contact so at least one person can help you recover information if something goes wrong.

- 记住自己住的酒店全名，不要只记“长得像那个白色楼”  
  Memorize the full name of your hotel instead of relying on visual memory alone.

#### Good habits / 日常使用建议

- 出门时只带当天真的会用到的证件  
  Carry only the documents you are actually likely to need that day.

- 护照不要和主手机、主银行卡塞在同一个小包里  
  Do not keep your passport, main phone, and main bank card in the same small bag.

- 在会场注册完成后，非必要不要一直把护照带在手里翻来翻去  
  Once you have checked in at the venue, do not keep handling your passport unless necessary.

- 酒店和会场地址最好离线保存，并额外备一份葡萄牙语版本，方便在没网/没翻译软件/没手机时直接给司机看  
  Save your hotel and venue addresses offline, and keep a Portuguese version as well so you can show it directly to a driver if you have no internet or translation tools.

#### If your passport is lost or stolen / 如果护照遗失或被盗

- 先确认是不是单纯放错地方，不要边走边慌着到处翻包  
  First confirm whether it is actually lost or just misplaced. Do not panic and search your bags in public while moving around.

- 尽快回到安全地点，整理你手头还有哪些证件和备份  
  Return to a safe place and work out which documents and backups you still have.

- 联系酒店、主办方或可信朋友，请他们协助你处理后续沟通  
  Contact your hotel, organizer, or a trusted friend and ask them to help with next-step logistics.

- 再联系使领馆或相关机构处理补办  
  Then contact the relevant embassy or consulate to handle replacement procedures.

### 1.5 Transport Plan / 交通方案

#### Bottom line / 一句话结论

**在巴西出行，默认用 Uber；提前准备好所有重要地址的英文和葡语文本；尽量不坐公共交通，也避免非必要步行；上车后尽早确认走收费快速路，不要抄近路。**

In Brazil, default to Uber; keep all important addresses ready in both English and Portuguese; avoid public transportation and unnecessary walking; confirm the toll expressway early and avoid shortcuts.

#### Recommended setup / 推荐配置

- 提前保存这几类地址的纯文本：机场、酒店、会场、常去餐厅、医院或紧急备用地点  
  Save plain-text versions of these addresses in advance: airport, hotel, venue, restaurants you may revisit, and one hospital or emergency backup location.

- 每个重要地址都准备两版：英文版和葡萄牙语版  
  Keep two versions of each important address: one in English and one in Portuguese.

- 默认提前装好并登录 Uber，不要等落地后临时注册、绑卡或找验证码  
  Install and log into Uber before departure instead of setting it up after landing.

- 提前准备一句给司机看的葡语：  
  `Por favor, vá pela via expressa com pedágio, sem atalhos.`（请走收费快速路，不要抄近路）  
  Prepare one Portuguese sentence to show the driver:  
   `Por favor, vá pela via expressa com pedágio, sem atalhos.` (“Please take the toll expressway and avoid shortcuts.”)

#### Before you leave / 出发前先做完

- 把酒店和会场地址保存为纯文字，不要只留截图。
  Save your hotel and venue addresses as plain text, not just screenshots.

- 把地址离线保存到手机里，确保没网时也能打开  
  Save the addresses offline on your phone so they remain accessible without internet.

- 把 Uber 绑好支付方式，并确认自己在海外也能正常登录  
  Add your payment method to Uber and make sure you can still log in abroad.

- 想清楚第一天最可能用到的两条路线：机场到酒店、酒店到会场  
  Work out the two routes you are most likely to need on day one: airport to hotel, and hotel to venue.

#### Default habits / 默认习惯

- 大部分出行都按“室内安全点到室内安全点”来规划  
  Plan most trips as movement from one safe indoor point to another.

- 能直接打车就不要改成公共交通；能少走一段就不要多走  
  If you can take a car directly, do not switch to public transit; if you can walk less, do not walk more.

- 尽量在酒店门口、商场门口、会场门口这种明确位置上下车  
  Use clear pickup and drop-off points such as hotel entrances, mall entrances, or venue entrances whenever possible.

- 尽量在室内或有前台、门卫的地方等车，不要长时间站在路边看手机  
  Wait indoors or near a front desk or guard whenever possible instead of standing on the street using your phone.

#### In the car / 上车后怎么做

- 先核对车牌、车型和司机名字，再上车  
  Check the plate number, car model, and driver name before getting in.

- 上车后尽早确认路线重点：走收费快速路，不要抄近路  
  Confirm the route early in the ride: use the toll expressway and avoid shortcuts.

- 如果司机需要确认地址，直接出示葡语文本，不要临场口头硬解释  
  If the driver needs the address confirmed, show the Portuguese text instead of trying to explain it verbally on the spot.

- 看一眼导航大方向即可，不需要一路自己指挥司机  
  Check the general route direction once, but do not feel the need to direct the driver throughout the trip.

#### High-risk cases / 特别注意的场景

- 从 GIG 机场出来去 Barra，不要把它当成普通的“上车就行”路线。机场一侧的主要通道靠近里约北区多片高风险区域，导航如果贪快、抄近路或下到地面支路，会让你暴露在更复杂的路口和停滞环境里  
  Do not treat a GIG-to-Barra ride as a routine “just get in and go” trip. The airport-side access corridors are close to several high-risk areas in Rio’s north zone, and a shortcut or surface-road detour can expose you to more complex intersections and stationary traffic.

- 这也是为什么要尽早和司机确认走收费快速路：重点不是“收费”本身，而是尽量把车程留在主干 expressway 上，少走地面路，少停红绿灯，少给人从路口接近车辆的机会  
  This is why you should confirm the toll expressway early in the ride. The point is not the toll itself, but keeping more of the trip on major expressways, with less time on local roads, fewer traffic lights, and fewer chances for someone to approach a stopped vehicle.

- 直接给司机看这句话：`Por favor, vá pela via expressa com pedágio, sem atalhos.` 意思是“请走收费快速路，不要抄近路”  
  Show the driver this sentence directly: `Por favor, vá pela via expressa com pedágio, sem atalhos.` It means: “Please take the toll expressway and avoid shortcuts.”

- 如果导航明显离开主干快速路、开始频繁进入地面道路或连续经过路口，就要提高警惕；你不需要一路指挥司机，但至少要确认大方向没有变成“为了省几分钟而走近路”  
  If the route clearly leaves the main expressway and starts using local roads with frequent intersections, pay closer attention. You do not need to direct the driver constantly, but you should make sure the trip has not turned into a shortcut just to save a few minutes.

#### Do not / 不要这样做

- 不要把公共交通当默认方案  
  Do not treat public transportation as your default option.

- 不要为了省一点钱，在不熟的区域长距离步行  
  Do not take long walks through unfamiliar areas just to save a little money.

- 不要在机场外、会场外拖着行李长时间站着看路线  
  Do not stand outside the airport or venue with your luggage while checking directions for a long time.

- 不要把“avoid tolls”当成省钱技巧  
  Do not treat “avoid tolls” as a clever money-saving option.

- 不要依赖临场口头沟通地址；地址要先输进 app，也要准备葡语版本备用  
  Do not rely on saying the address out loud on the spot; enter it in the app first and keep a Portuguese version as backup.

#### Notes / 备注

- 对语言不通的外国人来说，Uber 的价值首先在于目的地可以提前输入，不需要上车后再解释  
  For a foreign visitor who does not speak the language, Uber helps first by letting you enter the destination before the ride instead of explaining it after getting in.

- 出租车不是首选；只有在 Uber 不可用时，才考虑机场、酒店或商场安排的正规出租车，并直接出示葡语地址  
  Taxis are not the first choice; only use an official taxi arranged by the airport, hotel, or mall when Uber is not available, and show the Portuguese address directly.

### 1.6 Packing & Carry Strategy / 随身携带策略

#### Bottom line / 一句话结论

不要把“准备好了”理解成“全都带在身上”。出门时只带当天真会用到的东西；手机、护照、主卡、现金不要放在同一个包里，也不要让一个包被拿走就等于整套系统一起丢。

Do not confuse “prepared” with “carrying everything on you.” Only bring what you will actually need that day; do not keep your phone, passport, main card, and cash in the same bag, and do not let one stolen bag take down your entire system at once.

#### Recommended setup / 推荐配置

- 身上只保留当天要用的核心物品：手机、少量现金、1 张主卡、会场需要的证件或门票  
  Keep only the essentials you will actually use that day on you: your phone, a small amount of cash, one main card, and any ID or event pass you need.

- 备用卡、备用现金、额外证件尽量留在住处  
  Leave backup cards, backup cash, and extra documents in your room whenever possible.

- 不要把护照、主手机、主卡和全部现金塞进同一个小包  
  Do not put your passport, main phone, main card, and all your cash into the same small bag.

- 电脑、iPad、相机之类的高价值物品，只有当天真需要时再带  
  Only bring high-value items like a laptop, iPad, or camera when you actually need them that day.

#### What to carry on a normal conference day / 普通参会日带什么

- 手机
  Phone

- 少量现金
  A small amount of cash

- 1 张主卡
  One main card

- 会场证件 / 报名信息 / 酒店房卡
  Event pass or registration info, and your hotel key card

- 酒店和会场地址的离线文本
  Offline text versions of your hotel and venue addresses

#### What to leave behind when possible / 能不带就别带什么

- 备用银行卡
  Backup bank cards

- 大额现金
  Large amounts of cash

- 不会当天用到的证件
  Documents you will not need that day

- 护照原件（如果当天场景不需要）
  Your original passport, if you do not need it that day

- 额外的电子设备
  Extra electronics

#### Conference bag habits / 会场包的使用习惯

- 包越简单越好，不要一层层塞满贵重物品  
  Keep your bag simple. Do not load it with multiple layers of valuables.

- 在会场里也不要习惯性把包敞着、挂在椅背上或随手放地上  
  Even inside the venue, do not leave your bag open, hanging on a chair, or casually on the floor.

- 如果需要临时离开座位，能带走的核心物品尽量带走  
  If you step away from your seat, take your core valuables with you whenever possible.

- 不要因为“大家都在参会”就默认周围环境完全没风险  
  Do not assume the environment is risk-free just because everyone seems to be attending the conference.

#### Notes / 备注

- 很多人不是没准备，而是把准备好的东西全放在同一个包里  
  Many people are not underprepared; they simply put everything they prepared into the same bag.

- 出门前问自己一句：“如果这个包现在没了，我还能不能回酒店、联系别人、继续行程？”  
  Ask yourself one question before leaving: “If this bag disappeared right now, could I still get back to my hotel, contact someone, and continue the trip?”

### 1.7 Health, Medication & Basics / 药品与基础准备

#### Bottom line / 一句话结论

不要把身体状态当成小事。出发前把常用药、补水、充电和最基础的应急物品准备好，避免把自己弄到又饿、又渴、又没电、又找不到药的被动状态。

Do not treat your physical condition as a minor issue. Prepare your usual medication, hydration, charging, and a few basic emergency items before departure so you do not end up tired, dehydrated, out of battery, and without what you need.

#### Recommended setup / 推荐配置

- 常用药带够全程，并留一点缓冲，不要刚好卡在最后一天  
  Bring enough of your usual medication for the full trip, with a small buffer instead of cutting it exactly to the last day.

- 处方药尽量保留原包装，重要药物最好随身带，不要全塞进行李箱  
  Keep prescription medication in original packaging when possible, and carry important medication with you instead of putting everything in checked luggage.

- 每天随身带最基础的几样：水、充电线或充电宝、纸巾、少量零食  
  Carry a few basics each day: water, a charging cable or power bank, tissues, and a small snack.

- 如果你有任何“缺了就很麻烦”的东西，按备用思路准备一份  
  If there is anything that would seriously disrupt your day if lost or unavailable, prepare a backup for it.

#### What to bring / 建议带什么

- 常用处方药  
  Regular prescription medication

- 常见非处方药：止痛药、肠胃药、过敏药、晕车药（按个人需要）  
  Basic over-the-counter medication such as pain relief, stomach medicine, allergy medicine, or motion-sickness medicine, depending on your needs

- 充电线、转换头、充电宝  
  Charging cable, plug adapter, and power bank

- 可重复使用水瓶或随手能补水的容器  
  A reusable water bottle or another easy way to carry water

- 少量耐放零食  
  A small amount of shelf-stable snacks

#### Daily basics / 每天最基础的底线

- 不要长时间空腹出门  
  Do not go out for long periods on an empty stomach.

- 出门前看一眼电量，不要默认自己一定能很快找到充电的地方  
  Check your battery before leaving instead of assuming you will quickly find somewhere to charge.

- 白天补水，晚上回酒店前也尽量别把自己耗到太累  
  Stay hydrated during the day, and do not push yourself so far that you return to the hotel exhausted and depleted.

- 如果你当天会很晚结束，提前准备一点吃的和回程所需电量  
  If you expect a late finish, prepare some food and enough battery for the ride back in advance.

#### Medication habits / 药品使用习惯

- 每天固定吃的药尽量不要因为行程打乱  
  Do not let travel disrupt medication you take on a regular schedule.

- 药放在你知道的位置，不要临时要吃时才发现找不到  
  Keep medication somewhere you can reliably find it instead of discovering at the last minute that it has disappeared into your luggage.

- 如果你对某种药非常依赖，最好额外备一小份  
  If you depend heavily on a particular medication, keep a small backup supply.

#### Do not / 不要这样做

- 不要默认会场附近一定方便买到你熟悉的药  
  Do not assume you will easily find the medicine you know near the venue.

- 不要把补水、进食、充电拖到“等会儿再说”  
  Do not postpone hydration, food, or charging until it becomes a problem.

- 不要在已经很累、很饿、很渴、手机又快没电的时候再安排复杂移动  
  Do not plan complicated travel when you are already tired, hungry, dehydrated, and low on battery.

## 2. Arrival / 落地后

### 2.1 First Hour After Landing / 落地后第一小时

#### Bottom line / 一句话结论

落地后的第一目标不是“赶紧出发”，而是先把网络、目的地、打车和联系人这四件事稳住。不要拖着行李站在路边一边查路线一边做决定  

Your first goal after landing is not to leave as fast as possible, but to stabilize four things first: internet, destination, ride, and contacts. Do not stand outside with your luggage making decisions while checking directions.

#### First priorities / 第一优先级

- 先确认手机有网，能正常打开 Uber、地图和消息软件  
  First make sure your phone has internet and can open Uber, maps, and messaging apps.

- 确认酒店地址和会场地址能离线打开  
  Make sure your hotel and venue addresses are available offline.

- 给同行人或可信联系人发一句“我已落地”  
  Send a quick “I landed” message to a travel companion or trusted contact.

- 在室内把接下来的路线想清楚，再往外走  
  Decide on your next move while still indoors, then head outside.

#### Do not / 不要这样做

- 不要一出机场就站在路边研究去哪儿  
  Do not step outside the airport and then start figuring out where to go.

- 不要拖着行李长时间找信号、找验证码、绑卡、注册 app  
  Do not spend a long time outside dealing with signal issues, verification codes, payment setup, or app registration.

- 不要让自己处于“地址没存好、手机没网、车也没叫上”的状态  
  Do not let yourself get stuck with no saved address, no internet, and no ride booked.
  
#### Special note for airport-to-Barra rides / 机场到 Barra 特别注意

- 机场到 Barra 不要当普通市内通勤处理；重点不是打什么车型，而是尽量留在主干快速路上，少走地面道路，少在红绿灯路口停留  
  Do not treat airport-to-Barra rides as routine city trips; the key is not the car category but staying on major expressways, minimizing local-road detours, and reducing time spent stopped at traffic lights.

- 在 app 里先输好目的地，车到了再去指定上车点；上车后尽早确认走收费快速路，不要抄近路  
  Enter the destination in the app and confirm your car has arrived before going to the pickup point; once in the car, confirm the toll expressway early and avoid shortcuts.

- 如果司机需要确认路线或地址，直接出示葡语版本：`Por favor, vá pela via expressa com pedágio, sem atalhos.`（请走收费快速路，不要抄近路）。
  If the driver needs the route or address confirmed, show the Portuguese version directly: `Por favor, vá pela via expressa com pedágio, sem atalhos.` (“Please take the toll expressway and avoid shortcuts.”)

- 如果路线明显开始频繁下到地面道路或连续经过路口，要提高警惕  
  If the route clearly starts dropping onto local roads or passing frequent intersections, pay closer attention.

### 2.2 Hotel Check-in & First Night / 入住与第一晚

#### Bottom line / 一句话结论

第一晚的任务不是继续折腾，而是把自己和第二天都安顿好：入住、充电、补水、整理第二天要带的东西。非必要不要晚上再出门。  

The first night is not for doing more. It is for getting yourself and the next day in order: check in, charge your devices, rehydrate, and set aside what you need for tomorrow. Avoid unnecessary night trips.

#### What to do / 具体怎么做

- 确认酒店名称、地址和房间号，并保证自己离线也能找到  
  Confirm your hotel name, address, and room number, and make sure you can access them offline.

- 给同行人或可信联系人发一句“我已到酒店”    
  Send a quick “I arrived at the hotel” message to a travel companion or trusted contact.

- 给手机和充电宝充电，补水，吃一点东西  
  Charge your phone and power bank, rehydrate, and eat something.

- 把第二天要用的东西单独整理出来：主卡、少量现金、会场证件、酒店房卡  
  Set aside what you need for the next day: your main card, a small amount of cash, your event pass, and your hotel key card.

- 再看一眼第二天路线：怎么去会场，怎么回酒店  
  Review the next day’s route once: how to get to the venue and how to get back.

#### Do not / 不要这样做  

- 不要深夜临时出门探索或换地方  
  Do not go exploring late at night or change locations on impulse.

- 不要把第二天要用的关键物品分散在很多地方  
  Do not scatter tomorrow’s essentials across multiple places.

- 不要在没吃饭、没充电、没确认路线的情况下直接睡过去  
  Do not go straight to sleep without eating, charging, and confirming your route.

### 2.3 First Conference Morning / 第一个参会早晨

#### Bottom line / 一句话结论

第一天早上不要把自己搞得手忙脚乱。提前出门，少带东西，默认打 Uber 去会场，不要临场一边找证件一边看路线。

Do not make your first conference morning chaotic. Leave early, carry less, default to Uber to the venue, and avoid looking for documents and directions at the same time.

#### What to do / 具体怎么做

- 出门前确认四样东西：手机、主信用卡、少量现金、会场证件
  Before leaving, confirm four things: your phone, main credit card, a small amount of cash, and your event pass.

- 提前打开会场地址和 Uber，不要下楼后再慢慢处理
  Open the venue address and Uber before going downstairs.

- 预留一点冗余时间，尤其是第一天
  Leave yourself some buffer time, especially on the first day.

- 到会场后先确认注册、洗手间、咖啡和主要出口的位置
  Once at the venue, locate registration, restrooms, coffee, and the main exits first.

#### Do not / 不要这样做

- 不要第一天就背太多东西去会场
  Do not carry too much to the venue on day one.

- 不要因为“看起来不远”就临时改成步行
  Do not switch to walking just because it looks close.

- 不要把签到、找人、找会场、买早餐都堆在最后十分钟
  Do not leave check-in, meeting people, finding rooms, and buying breakfast for the last ten minutes.

## 3. Daily Safety / 日常安全

#### Bottom line / 一句话结论

在外面要尽量减少暴露手机、首饰、手表、电脑和其他一眼就能被抢走或看出值钱的东西；不要站在路边做决定，也不要让自己看起来像刚到、没方向、东西很多的外国参会者。

When you are out, the goal is to reduce visible exposure of your phone, jewelry, watch, laptop, and anything else that looks easy to snatch or obviously valuable; do not make decisions on the street, and do not look like a newly arrived foreign conference attendee carrying a lot.

#### What to do / 具体怎么做

- 尽量把查路线、回消息、找餐厅这些动作放在室内完成  
  Do route checks, messaging, and restaurant searches indoors whenever possible.

- 走路时专心走路，不要边走边看手机  
  When walking, focus on walking instead of looking at your phone.

- 包尽量简单低调，背法以贴身、能随时控制住开口的位置为主  
  Keep your bag simple and low-profile, and carry it in a way that stays close to your body and under control.

- 会场外非必要不要一直挂着参会证，也不要让人一眼看出你是参会游客  
  Do not keep your conference badge visible outside the venue unless necessary, and do not make yourself instantly identifiable as a conference visitor.

- 如果需要停下来处理事情，先进酒店、商场、咖啡馆或有门卫的地方  
  If you need to stop and deal with something, move into a hotel, mall, café, or another staffed place first.

#### High-visibility items / 特别容易引人注意的东西

- 不要佩戴明显昂贵的项链、耳环、手链、戒指  
  Do not wear obviously expensive necklaces, earrings, bracelets, or rings.

- 不要把高价手表、奢侈包、显眼品牌购物袋当日常出街配置  
  Do not treat a luxury watch, designer bag, or visible premium shopping bag as part of your normal street setup.

- 可穿戴设备也要低调看待；如果它一眼看起来贵、亮、显眼，就会增加被盯上的概率  
  Treat wearables the same way; if they look expensive, bright, or noticeable at a glance, they increase your visibility as a target.

- 骑车、步行、上下车时，更不要让项链、耳机、手机挂在特别容易被一把拽走的位置  
  When biking, walking, or getting in and out of cars, do not leave necklaces, earbuds, or phones in positions where they can be grabbed in one motion.

#### Do not / 不要这样做

- 不要站在路边长时间举着手机看地图  
  Do not stand on the street holding up your phone to check maps for a long time.

- 不要一边拖着行李或背着电脑包，一边临场决定下一步去哪  
  Do not decide your next move on the spot while dragging luggage or carrying a laptop bag.

- 不要骑车、走路或等车时佩戴明显值钱、容易被拽走的首饰  
  Do not wear obviously valuable and easy-to-snag jewelry while biking, walking, or waiting for a ride.

- 不要把手机、钱包或耳机放在外侧口袋、敞口托特包或很松的外层位置  
  Do not keep your phone, wallet, or earbuds in an outer pocket, open tote, or another loose outer position.

- 不要让自己看起来像“第一次到这里、完全不知道方向、还带着很多贵重物品”的状态  
  Do not look obviously lost, overloaded, and visibly valuable all at once.

## 4. Emergency Recovery After Theft or Robbery / 被盗抢后的紧急自救
### Immediate actions / 第一时间动作
1. Do not resist.
2. Move to a safe indoor location.
3. Contact hotel staff, organizer, or a trusted friend.
4. Freeze bank cards immediately.
5. Lock your phone and revoke important sessions.

1. 不要反抗。
2. 尽快进入安全的室内地点。
3. 立刻联系酒店、主办方或可信朋友。
4. 立即冻结银行卡。
5. 锁定手机并退出重要账号登录。

### If your phone is stolen / 如果手机被抢
- Mark the device as lost
- Change your email password first
- Then change banking, messaging, and social app passwords
- Revoke important sessions

- 将设备标记为丢失
- 优先修改邮箱密码
- 再修改银行、通讯和社交软件密码
- 退出重要账号登录

### If your passport is stolen / 如果护照被盗
- Confirm whether you still have a digital copy
- Contact your embassy or consulate
- Ask hotel or organizer for help with local logistics

- 确认是否保留护照电子副本
- 联系使领馆
- 请酒店或主办方协助处理本地沟通
